What is Bootstrap?

Bootstrap provides a base styling for almost every common web interfaces. They also includes few good JavaScript plugins, usually with almost no additional work required, except HTML markup.

Bootstrap are designed to be responsive from the beginning. You can be assured that your website will look good in any devices.

Once you integrate Bootstrap, extending your site, or building additional pages are a breeze. This allows you to save on development time, and focus your resources on something else more important, such as your content.
